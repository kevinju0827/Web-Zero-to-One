Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with the grated cheese.
  4. Combine pasta, pancetta, and the egg mixture off the heat.
