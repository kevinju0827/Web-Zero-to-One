Homemade Pancakes

This simple pancake recipe is perfect for a warm breakfast or brunch. It is easy to prepare and use ingredients you may already have at home.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients and measure them out before you begin.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, milk, and eggs.
  3. Whisk the mixture until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  4. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter.
  5. Pour a small scoop of batter into the pan and cook until bubbles appear on the surface.
  6. Flip the pancake and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.
